title:  "Black Hole Shreds Passing Star (Simulation)"
---

This rendering depicts a star being torn apart by the immense tidal forces of
a massive black hole. Much of the stellar debris is sucked into the
singularity while some matter is flung outwards at very high speeds.  
  
This animation was based off a real event (ASASSN-14li) observed by NASA's
Chandra X-ray Observatory, Swift Gamma-ray Burst Explorer, and ESA/NASA's XMM-
Newton in November 2014.  
  
Source: <https://youtu.be/hu6hIhW00Fk> (NASA Goddard)
